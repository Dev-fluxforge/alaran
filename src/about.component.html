Who We Are

Charting the Future with Precision and Integrity

Since 2008, Alaran Geo-Service has been a cornerstone of infrastructural development, providing world-class surveying and geospatial services that blend cutting-edge technology with decades of expertise.

Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders

Founded by Dr. Adebayo Alaran with a single theodolite and a vision for excellence, our firm has grown into a multi-disciplinary team of licensed experts. We've embraced technological advancements, from the first commercial GPS units to today's sophisticated drone LiDAR systems, always with the goal of delivering more accurate data, faster.

Our portfolio spans hundreds of successful projects across Nigeria and West Africa, from landmark high-rise buildings to critical energy infrastructure. We are proud to be the trusted geospatial partner for both public and private sector giants.
